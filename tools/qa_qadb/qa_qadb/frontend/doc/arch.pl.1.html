SYNOPSIS

       arch.pl

DESCRIPTION

       arch.pl  guesses machine's location.  The location is based on the out-
       put of uname -m

       If running under Xen, xen0- is prefixed in the case of Dom0,  xenU-  in
       the case of DomU.

       All i386, i486, i586, and i686 are reported as i586.
